🌙 MISSION 3 SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
================================

## 📁 FILES NEEDED:
1. night.png - The dark/night background image for Mission 3

## 🔐 PASSWORD SETUP:
Open script.js and find this line (around line 750):
   let passwordCorrect = ""; // Set this to your password

Change it to your password, for example:
   let passwordCorrect = "BINI2024";

## 💌 YOUR LETTER:
Find this section in script.js (around line 755):
   const yourLetter = `Dear [Name],
   
   [Write your letter here]
   ...`;

Replace it with your actual letter. Example:
   const yourLetter = `Dear [Her Name],

Happy Birthday! 🎂

I hope you enjoyed this little adventure I made for you.
You mean so much to me and I wanted to create something
special to celebrate your day.

Thank you for being amazing!

With all my love,
[Your Name]`;

## 📝 HINTS IN JOURNAL:
The hints section in the journal already exists!
Players can press J to open the journal and see hints.

To add hints, they're in the journal's "Hints" section.
The password box will tell players to check the journal for hints.

## 🎮 HOW MISSION 3 WORKS:

1. After completing Mission 2, player heads to the door
2. Scene changes to night.png (dark room)
3. Player walks to the CENTER of the room
4. Password box appears with:
   - Password input field
   - 10-minute countdown timer
   - Hint: "Check the hints in your journal"
5. Player can:
   - Enter the correct password → Box opens immediately
   - Wait 10 minutes → Box opens automatically
   - Close the box and come back (timer keeps running)
6. When box opens → Your letter appears
7. After reading letter → Game complete message!
